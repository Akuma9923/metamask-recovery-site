Recover Your Metamask Account

If you lost access to your Metamask account, follow the steps below to recover it:

  1. Step 1: Open Metamask
  2. Step 2: Click on "Settings"
  3. Step 3: Click on "Security & Privacy"
  4. Step 4: Click on "Reveal Seed Phrase"
  5. Step 5: Enter your password and click "Continue"
  6. Step 6: Write down your 12-word seed phrase and keep it safe!
